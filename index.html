Our Channels

DevCo is a band that makes songs for novels and screenplays. Our first novel "Dimension" will be released in December 2025.

The novel is loosely based on our album "Pop Song Summer" which is being released as a series of singles before and after the book launch. Many of the characters are embedded in the songs, and the story is revealed progressively across our platforms.

Follow DevCo across different platforms to experience different aspects of our story. Each channel offers unique content that contributes to the unfolding narrative.

About DevCo

Born from code, fueled by chaos

In the void between digital and analog, we emerged. Not as musicians, but as anomalies in the system. Our sound—a glitch in the matrix of conventional music.

We don't create; we compile. We don't perform; we execute. Each track is an algorithm of emotion, each beat a binary pulse of existence.

The future isn't coming. It's compiling in our studio, one error message at a time.

"The sound of tomorrow's yesterday"
